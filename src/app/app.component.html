About this place

Perfect getaway from the busy city! Feel relaxed while looking at the view of Mt. Banahaw from the bedroom. Take a dip in our mini pool while you enjoy the panoramic view of nature. You may pitch a tent on our garden and stargaze on the clear sky. Listen to the sound of nature as the silence of your surroundings sooth your ears.

The space
-Our villa has 1 br with loft that has mattresses
-We are located in the mountains so expect nature sounds (crickets at night and birds in the morning)
-Water source is from natural spring and is limited. Expect natural sediments in pool (small rocks etc.) but rest assured that this is filtered and clean.
-Expect flying insects at night. To repel, our caretaker can help you create smoke using dried coconut shells. Also, citronella plants surround our villa as preventive from other insects.
-Since we don't have the dirty kitchen facility yet, you may bring food and we offer cooking services by for 300 php (whole stay) this amount goes directly to the locals

Guest access
Upon SLEX Sto. Tomas exit you will pass by All home, SM San Pablo and Puregold. There's also 711, Taza Mia, BigMac and Angel's burger before you enter the barangay. Small sari-sari store is available just ask our caretaker.

No rough roads going to the villa so you can drive up easily :)

Parking lot is available which can fit up to 3-4 cars
